About Us

From Our Kitchen to Yours: The Story of Kulcheywala

Our journey began in the bustling streets of Amritsar, where the art of making perfect kulcha has been passed down through generations. At Kulcheywala, we bring you the authentic taste of Punjab with every bite.

Using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients, our skilled chefs craft each kulcha with love and precision. From the perfectly spiced potato filling to the crispy, golden exterior, every element is carefully prepared to deliver an unforgettable culinary experience.

We believe that great food brings people together, and our mission is to share the warmth and richness of Indian cuisine with food lovers everywhere.

Main Dish

Heavenly Sip: Amritsari Kulcha with Choley

Indulge in our signature Amritsari Kulcha, a crispy and flaky bread stuffed with spiced potatoes and onions, served alongside our rich and flavorful choley (chickpea curry). This classic combination is a true taste of Punjab.

Our kulcha is baked to perfection in a traditional tandoor, giving it that authentic smoky flavor and crispy texture that melts in your mouth. Paired with our aromatic choley, it's a meal that will transport you straight to the streets of Amritsar.

Drinks

Heavenly Sip: The Ultimate Refreshing Delight

Complete your meal with our traditional masala chai, brewed with a perfect blend of aromatic spices including cardamom, cinnamon, and ginger. This refreshing beverage is the perfect complement to our spicy dishes.

Made with premium tea leaves and fresh milk, our chai is simmered to perfection, creating a rich and creamy texture with just the right balance of sweetness and spice. It's more than just a drink ‚Äì it's a warm embrace in a cup.
